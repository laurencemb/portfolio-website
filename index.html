About Me

Hi I'm Laurence, a front-end developer based in South London. I have recently retrained following a career within the fashion industry. In addition to my front-end development skills I have a strong interest in User Experience, and I am a qualified UX designer. Now I enjoy applying knowledge of user psychology to design and build well rounded products which feel effortless to use and which customers will return to time and again.

​ In my free time, you can find me exploring my creative passions of printing and sewing, or broadening my horizons in galleries and museums. I love live music, theatre and comedy. I’m no stranger to a long hike through the countryside. I can find the joy in pretty much anything!
